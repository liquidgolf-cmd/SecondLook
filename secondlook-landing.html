Minimal interruptions. Nora appears only for clear scam patterns — payment requests, crypto instructions, or arrest threats. Perfect for independent users who want a light safety net.

The default. Nora flags suspicious patterns, unusual urgency, and pressure language. Offers context before you act. Suggested for most users.
